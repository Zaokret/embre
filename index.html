About

Embre is a vibrant and close-knit community that was founded in late 2020 by Sam Perrin. Despite the recent creation, the community has come a long way and is prided upon the diverse and experienced range of producers, selectors and curators that are present within the group. Embre aims to showcase an array of unique and colourful sounds from talented and up-and-coming members from within our community, recently made a reality via our brand new Embre Series and weekly ''Sunday16'' producing challenge.

Admins:

Sam Perrin | Stu Kelly | Blayr | Mario Tavares
